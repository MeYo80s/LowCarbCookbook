# Crispy Lemon-Garlic Chicken Thighs with Caper Butter

![Crispy Lemon-Garlic Chicken Thighs with Caper Butter](../../assets/recipes/crispy-lemon-garlic-chicken-thighs-caper-butter.jpg)

**Serves:** 4  
**Estimated net carbs:** ~2g per serving
**Estimated macros:** ~515 cal | 32g protein | 41g fat | 3g carbs

### Ingredients
- 8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (about 2 1/2 lb)
- 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp capers, drained
- 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1 tsp lemon zest
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley (optional)

### Optional Add-Ins
- Pinch of red pepper flakes
- 1 tbsp grated parmesan to finish

### Instructions
1. Pat chicken very dry with paper towels. Season both sides with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
2. Place thighs skin-side down in a cold skillet, then set heat to medium. Cook 12-15 minutes until most fat renders and skin is deep golden.
3. Flip and cook 8-10 minutes more until internal temp is 175-185 F for tender dark meat.
4. Move chicken to a plate and pour off all but 1 tbsp fat.
5. Reduce heat to low. Add butter and minced garlic; stir 30 seconds.
6. Add capers, lemon juice, lemon zest, and optional red pepper flakes. Simmer 30-60 seconds.
7. Return chicken skin-side up. Spoon sauce around (not over) skin to keep it crisp.
8. Finish with parsley and optional parmesan. Serve immediately.

### Notes
- This recipe avoids yogurt marinades so flavor stays rich, not sour.
- For best texture, do not overcrowd the skillet.

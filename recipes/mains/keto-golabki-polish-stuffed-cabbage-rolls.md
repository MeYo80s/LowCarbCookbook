# Traditional Keto Golabki (Polish Stuffed Cabbage Rolls)

**Serves:** 8  
**Estimated net carbs:** ~6g per serving
**Estimated macros:** ~380 cal | 28g protein | 24g fat | 9g carbs

### Ingredients
- 1 large head green or Savoy cabbage
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 lb ground pork
- 2 cups cauliflower rice (raw or lightly sauteed)
- 1 small onion, finely diced and sauteed
- 1 large egg
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp dried marjoram
- 2 cups sugar-free marinara or tomato sauce
- 1 cup beef or chicken broth
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar (optional)

### Optional Add-Ins
- Sour cream for serving
- Fresh dill or parsley, chopped

### Instructions
1. Heat oven to 350 F.
2. Core the cabbage and submerge the whole head in boiling salted water for 5-8 minutes. Peel off leaves as they soften.
3. Trim the thick center vein on the back of each leaf with a paring knife.
4. In a large bowl, mix ground beef, ground pork, cauliflower rice, sauteed onion, egg, garlic, salt, pepper, and marjoram until combined.
5. Place 2-3 tbsp filling near the stem end of each leaf. Fold sides inward and roll tightly.
6. Spread a thin layer of sauce in a 9x13 baking dish. Optional: line pan bottom with torn extra cabbage leaves.
7. Arrange rolls seam-side down. Stir broth into remaining sauce (and vinegar if using), then pour over rolls.
8. Cover tightly with foil and bake 60-90 minutes, until meat is cooked through and cabbage is tender.

### Instant Pot Option
1. Layer torn extra cabbage leaves on the bottom of the pot.
2. Arrange rolls seam-side down and pour sauce + broth mixture over top.
3. Cook on High Pressure for 18-20 minutes, then natural release 5 minutes.

### Notes
- Marjoram gives this dish its classic Polish flavor profile.
- Cabbage softness varies by head size and age; add time if leaves are still firm.
